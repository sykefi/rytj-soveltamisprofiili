This variant of the arc simply stores the parameters of the second constructor of the component GM_Arcs and recalculates the other attributes of the standard arc. The controlPoint sequence is similar to that in GM_ArcString, but the midPoint GM_Position is not needed since it is to be calculated. The control point sequence shall consist of the start and end points of each arc.<br/>
